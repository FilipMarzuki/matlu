Matlu Licensing Notice
======================

Matlu uses split licensing:

- Source code is licensed under the MIT License. See `LICENSE`.
- Original Matlu assets are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC-BY 4.0). See `LICENSE-ASSETS.md`.
- Third-party assets keep their original licenses and attribution requirements.

For source packs under `public/assets/packs/`, the generated `public/assets/manifest.json`
lists the files available to the game. Third-party packs with their own license files are
not relicensed by Matlu; check the license file shipped inside that pack before reusing,
redistributing, or monetizing those assets.

Do not add third-party assets to the repository unless their license is ship-safe for this
project, documented alongside the asset, and compatible with the intended use. In
particular, avoid CC-BY-NC or other non-commercial-only licenses for assets that ship with
the game.
